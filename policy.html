Store Policies

1. Return & Exchange Policy

We accept returns and exchanges within 7 days of delivery. Items must be:

  • Unworn and unused
  • In their original packaging
  • With original tags attached

To initiate a return, please email us at support@yourstore.com with your order number.

2. Shipping Policy

We deliver across India within 5–7 business days. Orders are shipped within 24–48 hours of confirmation.

Tracking details will be sent to your registered email/SMS once dispatched.

3. Payment Policy

We accept payments via:

  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • Credit/Debit Cards
  • Net Banking
  • Cash on Delivery (for select pin codes)

All transactions are secure and encrypted via Razorpay.

4. Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. Your personal information will only be used to process orders and provide updates. We never share your data with third parties.

5. Cancellation Policy

Orders can be canceled before they are shipped. Please contact us at support@yourstore.com or via WhatsApp within 12 hours of placing the order.
